Fall Foliage

Why venture all the way to New England? Autumn's splendor is right here at the Garden. From maples to oaks to beeches to unexpected surprises (like the cutleaf staghorn sumac, with its fuzzy red fruit against a backdrop of slender orange leaves), you'll see everything that's glorious about fall. Come immerse yourself in all the spectacular colors of the season.
